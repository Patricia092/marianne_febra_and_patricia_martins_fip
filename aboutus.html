About Us

Behind The Name:

Živa is the revered goddess at the heart of our brand’s captivating story, drawing inspiration from ancient Slavic folklore.

Interwoven into the original folklore of ZIMA itself, Živa emerges as an enigmatic and powerful goddess, bestowing her profound significance upon the heart of ZIMA.

In every sip of ZIMA, the legacy of Živa’s blessing endures, connecting you to the hypnotic prosperity of a bygone era.

Concocting Perfection:

Committed to ethical practices, ZIMA embraces fair trade sourcing for its product, ensuring a positive impact on the communities and environments involved.

The brand actively supports fair wages and safe working conditions for farmers and laborers, fostering economic empowerment and social development within the regions of cultivation.

Additionally, ZIMA promotes environmentally responsible practices, encouraging farmers to adopt sustainable agricultural methods that reduce carbon footprints and preserve biodiversity.

By prioritizing fair trade sourcing, ZIMA not only delivers a delightful and refreshing beverage but also contributes to a more equitable and sustainable global supply chain.

Our Promise & Legacy

ZIMA is deeply committed to making a positive impact on society through its charitable efforts. The company actively collaborates with various non-profit organizations, supporting causes such as environmental conservation, education, and community development.

By donating a percentage of its profits, organizing fundraisers, and engaging in volunteer initiatives, ZIMA strives to uplift underprivileged communities and contribute to meaningful change.

Building upon this philanthropic legacy, ZIMA is excited to introduce its new legacy flavor, “Moonlight Oasis”. With every purchase of this exquisite citrus-inspired beverage, a portion of the proceeds will be dedicated to expanding educational opportunities in underserved regions.

Through this wonderful initiative, ZIMA not only satisfies consumers’ palates but also empowers the future generation, making every sip of “Moonlight Oasis” a refreshing step towards a brighter and more equitable tomorrow.
